# Intelligence Calibration Val D Core

`Točka 5 / Val D` adds the bounded `Defensive Simulation & Quality Measurement Gate` layer on top of active `Val 0`, `Val A`, `Val B`, and `Val C` intelligence calibration proofs.

Val D is fail-closed on:
- `Val 0` calibration discipline foundation
- `Val A` reachability and VEX calibration
- `Val B` behavioral baseline and learning mode
- `Val C` feedback, suppression, and federated tuning

Val D covers:
- defensive simulation scenario harness
- adversarial and low-signal scenario library
- missed-detection analysis
- false-positive / false-negative balance review
- confidence calibration review
- VEX candidate quality review
- reachability calibration quality review
- behavioral calibration quality review
- federated weighting quality review
- simulation coverage review
- intelligence quality scoreboard

Val D remains bounded:
- simulation results, reviews, coverage summaries, and scoreboards remain advisory/projection-only
- no simulation result or quality metric mutates enforcement, suppression, priority, VEX publication, or local evidence
- quality measurement stays scoped to reviewed operational evidence and does not claim universal intelligence quality
- all outputs remain `projection_only` and `not_canonical_truth`

Val D does **not** complete `Točka 5`.

What remains for later waves:
- `Val E` integrated closure across the full intelligence calibration program
