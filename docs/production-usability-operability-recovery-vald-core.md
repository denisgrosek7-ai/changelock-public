# Production Usability, Operability & Recovery Hardening Val D Core

`Val D` turns the accepted `Val 0`, `Val A`, `Val B`, and `Val C` layers into the bounded `Final Usability Gate` slice for `Točka 4`.

The canonical rule does not change:

- one canonical truth, multiple projections
- the evidence spine remains canonical
- config inspection, explainability, dry-run, UI, CLI, API, readiness, health, diagnostics, support, export, and final-gate summaries remain projections only
- no `Val D` review output may become canonical truth or grant new mutation authority

## Val D Dependency

`Val D` is fail-closed on:

- `Val 0` foundation
- `Val A` config and explainability core
- `Val B` UI/API/CLI resilience
- `Val C` supportability and lifecycle operations

If any dependency is missing or inactive, `Val D` cannot become active.

## Val D Reviews

`Val D` adds bounded final-gate reviews for:

- config correctness
- explainability clarity
- dry-run and audit-only correctness
- permission-aware redaction
- degraded, stale, and partial behavior
- UI, data, windowing, and result semantics
- command center and noise budget behavior
- API protection, fairness, and backpressure
- CLI retry and idempotency posture
- supportability, readiness, diagnostics, and health
- recovery UX
- upgrade and rollback advisory posture
- production scale envelope
- governance and canonical-truth boundaries
- usability regression fixture coverage

Each review is proof-only. None of these reviews may mutate config, evidence, workflow, or governed runtime state.

## What Val D Checks

`Val D` verifies that:

- unsupported or unknown config and schema states cannot pass as usable
- explain outputs remain specific, bounded, and redaction-safe
- dry-run and audit-only stay non-mutating and non-authoritative
- partner/public-safe outputs cannot expose internal raw evidence
- stale, partial, degraded, unavailable, and unsupported states remain distinct
- windowed and cached views never overclaim completeness or canonical truth
- command-center and noise surfaces stay advisory-only and auditable
- API protection does not hide policy denials or bypass governance
- CLI retry and idempotency posture stays honest and fail-closed
- support bundles, diagnostics, health, and exports remain manifest-driven, redaction-safe, and projection-only
- recovery guidance remains guidance only and does not authorize bypasses
- upgrade and rollback advisory remains bounded, scoped, and non-mutating
- scale posture remains measurement-aware and limitation-bound
- no usability surface weakens evidence, policy, or governance discipline

## Why Val D Does Not Complete Točka 4

`Val D` proves only `Final Usability Gate` readiness.

It does **not** implement:

- integrated closure across the full point
- final `Točka 4 = PASS`
- final closure proof that all `Val 0/A/B/C/D` slices are jointly closed

That remains the scope of `Val E Integrated Closure`.
