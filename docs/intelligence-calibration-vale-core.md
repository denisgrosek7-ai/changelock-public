# Intelligence Calibration Val E Core

`Točka 5 / Val E` is the integrated closure for the intelligence calibration program.

Val E is fail-closed on:
- `Val 0` calibration foundation
- `Val A` reachability and VEX calibration
- `Val B` behavioral baseline and learning mode
- `Val C` feedback, suppression, and federated tuning
- `Val D` defensive simulation and quality measurement

Val E covers:
- integrated dependency closure
- cross-val coherence review
- final `Point 5` pass rule
- integrated advisory / canonical-truth boundary review
- integrated reachability / VEX safety review
- integrated behavioral / learning safety review
- integrated feedback / suppression / federated safety review
- integrated simulation / quality measurement review
- integrated regression closure
- integrated proofs surface

Val E is the only step that may mark `Točka 5` as PASS.

Val E remains bounded:
- it does not add new broad intelligence functionality
- it only connects and verifies `Val 0` through `Val D` as one coherent fail-closed system
- integrated closure remains advisory/projection-only and does not become canonical truth
- no integrated review mutates suppression, thresholds, priority, enforcement, federated propagation, or final VEX publication

`Točka 5` is PASS only when:
- `Val 0` through `Val D` are active/pass
- `Val E` dependency closure, coherence review, boundary review, safety reviews, simulation/quality review, and regression closure are all active/pass
- `point_5_state = intelligence_calibration_point_5_pass`
