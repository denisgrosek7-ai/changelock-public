## Boundaries

This slice does not claim:

- direct legal verdict authority
- self-issued certification
- insurer-facing export programs
- actuarial benchmark publication
- public widening of regulator-safe or certifier-safe verifier surfaces
- hidden institutional expansion through compliance automation

Compliance outputs remain bounded by:

- policy profile scope
- manual interpretation visibility
- audience narrowing
- evidence freeze and challenge workflow
- non-delegable release controls

## Deferred From This Slice

The following remain outside `Phase 8A` bounded closure:

- risk quantification baseline
- insurance-facing evidence exports
- incident attribution support
- actuarial benchmark discipline
- insurer integration program
- broader institutional expansion beyond compliance codification
