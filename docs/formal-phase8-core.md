## Boundaries

This slice does not claim:

- direct legal verdict authority
- regulator replacement authority
- insurer replacement authority
- certification issuance authority
- broad automated institutional mutation authority
- full institutional expansion completion

All outputs remain bounded by claim class, use-permission discipline, standard-of-proof discipline, evidence custody, and authority-routing controls.
